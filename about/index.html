About

Me

My name is Haitham Gad. I've been writing software for the past 7 years. I'm interested in all aspects of software development, from programming and algorithms to software design and verification.

My primary interest though, is the study of programming languages. I like to learn new languages, explore new ideas and utilitze different programming paradigms. I use C++ for my day job, but I've written considerable amounts of code in several other languages including Haskell, Python and Objective-C, in addition to other domain-specific languages like SKILL (a dialect of Lisp). I also happen to be interested in the foundations of computer science and its relation to mathematics and mathematical logic. As such, I often find myself fiddling with some esoteric kinds of tools and proof assistants like PARI/GP, Coq and Agda.

I'm originally from Cairo, Egypt and I currently live in North Side Pittsburgh. My formal education is in Electrical Engineering (earned a B.Sc. EE from Cairo University in 2006). I've been working at the Cairo offshore site of my former employer since I graduated until I moved to the company's headquarters in Oregon late 2009. In 2012, I started working at my new employer's local office in Pittsburgh.
