The Mind and its Education, by George Betts is one of those books with high signal-to-noise ratio.

Studying the mind can be done in three ways:

  1. Intospection: contemplating about our own thoughts;
  2. Text-book description: to know what to look for while introspecting;
  3. Observation of others: either uncritical or scientific.
