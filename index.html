Why start a blog?

October 27, 2013

Writing turns vague thoughts into tangible ideas. It often occurs that people think they understand some idea very well. But it’s not until they’re asked to express their thoughts that they find it hard to clearly formulate them. You cannot write down your thoughts until you’ve reached a level of understanding that allows you to express them with enough precision. As the proverb goes, the best way to learn something is to teach it.

Moreover, It’s much easier to recall ideas that you’ve taken the effort to illustrate in your own words than those that you’ve come across somewhere sometime in the past.

Most importantly, it’s not very useful to become a knowledge sink. It doesn’t matter how much you know so long as you don’t share this knowledge with others. Knowledge is only valuable insofar as it’s accessible. Moreover, sharing thoughts with others makes them subject to further scrutiny. This often helps the author perfect some ideas and rethink others.

I’ve been meaning to start this blog for quite some time, but was too lazy to get the show on the road. I was finally able to set aside some time and get it done. Hope you find my posts interesting, useful, or at least entertaining!
