Why start a blog?

October 27, 2013

Writing turns vague thoughts into tangible ideas. It often occurs that I think I understand some idea; but it’s not until I’m asked to express it that I find myself incapable of clearly formulating it. You cannot write down your thoughts until you’ve reached a level of understanding that allows you to express them with enough precision.

Moreover, I find it easier to judge an idea and turn it over in my head when I see it written down. Reading my own insights helps me spot the fallacies, fix the misconceptions and establish connections with other ideas.

Another advantage of recording insights (especially in a digital format), is that it’s easier to recall answers to questions which tend to crop up every once in a while.

Sharing my thoughts with others makes them subject to further scrutiny. The feedback I get from others helps me perfect some ideas and rethink others.

I’ve been meaning to start this blog for quite some time, but was too lazy to get the show on the road. I was finally able to set aside some time and get it done. Hope you find my insights interesting, useful, or at least entertaining!
